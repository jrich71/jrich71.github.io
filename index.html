Summary

I am a nationally recognized health informaticist with on-the-ground care delivery experience within multiple settings seeking to make everyday care better by connecting great people with fantastic tech. My experience and expertise span multiple biomedical domains–providing me a broad healthcare perspective–in developing, implementing, and evaluating digital interventions that impact decision-making and clinical workflows. Known as a highly effective collaborator in both operational and research environments, my vision brings together stakeholders on key issues: AI, health informatics, patient-centeredness, and data-driven decision making.

In my career I have embedded within clinical teams among a variety of care setttings to develop and evaluate ways to deliver knowledge-based resources and sevices to providers and patients. This has required of me to work with a variety of health data standards including FHIR, electronic clinical quality measures (eCQMs), clinical quality language (CQL), and more; as well as collaborate with a variety of stakeholders from clinicians to IT staff to information professionals/librarians. In addition to product development and rollout, I am highly-published and get energized when presenting lessons learned and research results.
